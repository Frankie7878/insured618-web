What is Term Life Insurance?

Term Life insurance is designed to provide robust financial security for a specific duration—typically 10, 20, or 30 years. If the insured passes away during the term, the nominated beneficiaries receive a completely tax-free lump sum benefit.

💲

High Affordability

Initial premiums are a fraction of permanent insurance costs, maximizing your immediate leverage.

🔒

Locked-in Premiums

Your monthly or annual premium rate is 100% guaranteed to remain fixed throughout your selected term.

⏳

Flexible Durations

Choose from comprehensive options spanning 10, 15, 20, 25, 30, or even 40 years.
