What is Participating Life Insurance?

Participating Life (Par Life) insurance is a robust model uniting "Lifelong Protection" with "Asset Accumulation".

Along with locked-in lifetime death benefits, it allows you to participate in the carrier's profit distributions (dividends) to accumulate tax-deferred compound cash values over time.

Why Select Participating Whole Life?

🛡️

Lifetime Guarantees

Regardless of stock market or interest rate volatility, your basic death benefit, base cash value, and premiums are 100% legally guaranteed by the carrier.

📈

Dividend Payout Potentials

While dividends are not guaranteed, top Canadian insurance giants (e.g. Sun Life) have paid active annual dividends to policyholders every single year since 1877.

💰

Tax-sheltered Wealth Growth

Cash values accumulate completely tax-deferred within the policy, establishing a stable asset class immune to stock market volatility.

💧

Flexible Capital Liquidity

Access accumulated cash values via policy loans or bank collateralization to fund your retirement, children's universities, or corporate investments.
