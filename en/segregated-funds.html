🌟 What are Segregated Funds?

Segregated Funds are unique investment structures provided exclusively by Canadian insurance companies. At their core, they operate similarly to standard Mutual Funds—pooling capital to diversify into professional equities and bonds. However, as an **individual variable insurance contract**, they build in powerful "safety nets," securing principal guarantees, probate bypass protections, and potential creditor shielding.

🛡️ Why Choose Segregated Funds?

Four Core Advantages Protecting Your Capital

💎

1. Uncompromising Principal Guarantees

(Principal Guarantees)

Regardless of market corrections, your investments secure a robust floor. Upon contract maturity or death, the carrier **guarantees to payout between 75% to 100% of your initial deposits** (net of withdrawals).

📈

2. Profit Locking "Reset" Feature

(Reset Option)

Not just protecting base capital, but locking in your growth! Many contracts allow you to reset your guarantee values annually (typically until age 80). **When market valuations increase, you lock in those gains as your new base guarantee floor**.

🕊️

3. Private and Rapid Wealth Transfer

(Estate Bypass & Probate-Free)

Unlike ordinary mutual funds, death benefits are **paid directly, quickly, and completely privately to your named beneficiaries**, bypassing complex and costly provincial probate courts.

🏛️

4. Potential Creditor Shielding

(Potential Creditor Protection)

An invaluable armor for business owners, medical professionals, and self-employed consultants. By naming a qualified family member as beneficiary, contract assets **may be fully insulated from creditor claims or legal judgments**.
