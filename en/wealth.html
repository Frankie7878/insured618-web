2. Balancing Asset Growth with Core Protection

Core Philosophy: Pursue market-linked capital growth to fight inflation while ensuring your primary principal remains legally protected.

Popular Choice
📈

Segregated Funds

Combines the wealth accumulation potential of standard mutual funds with the robust security features of insurance contracts.

  • ✓ Principal Guarantees: Offers a guaranteed return of 75% to 100% of your principal upon contract maturity or death, shielding you from market downturns.
  • ✓ Market Resets: Allows lock-in of market gains. As the market rises, your guaranteed value increases and locks in permanently.

🔒

Insurance GICs

Provides 100% principal and interest guarantees, custom-made for risk-averse wealth preservation.

  • ✓ Absolute Safety: Fully backed guarantees ensure your principal is safe regardless of any economic volatility.
  • ✓ Estate Advantages: Unlike bank GICs, insurance GICs bypass probate fees completely by naming direct beneficiaries.

3. Tax Optimization & Seamless Estate Settlement

It is not just about how much you make; it is about how much you keep and pass down.

⚖️

Bypassing Probate Fees

By nominating direct beneficiaries on your accounts, your assets transfer swiftly and privately to your heirs upon passing—bypassing lengthy, costly probate procedures and maintaining complete privacy.

📝

Legacy Settlement Payouts

You retain full control over how heirs receive their funds (e.g. distributed gradually over 10 years rather than a lump sum), preventing rapid depletion. Perfect for blended families or special needs heirs.

🏢

Corporate Owner Synergy

Leverage Corporate Asset Transfer models to direct taxable corporate surplus into tax-sheltered contracts, utilizing the CDA (Capital Dividend Account) to flow profits out completely tax-free.
