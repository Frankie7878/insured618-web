Our Story

Insured618 was founded on a simple belief: "Financial security should not be complex or intimidating."

Having lived in Canada for many years, we understand the unique challenges faced by immigrant families and business owners—from establishing their first health protection plan to setting up education funds for children and executing complex corporate tax-free wealth transfers. The market is saturated with complicated terminology and pushy sales tactics, but very few people truly sit down to listen to your story.

We decided to change that. As independent insurance and wealth advisors, we do not represent any single insurance company. We represent **you**. We source the absolute best options from across the entire Canadian market, translating them into simple, clear language to help you make empowered decisions.
