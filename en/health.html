Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How much does private health insurance cost in Canada?

A: Rates depend heavily on your age, province of residence, selected plan level, and (for personal health plans) health history. You can obtain a free quote online anytime.

Q: Does the insurance carrier pay my clinic or pharmacy directly?

A: Yes. Your health card acts as a Pay-Direct Card. In-network pharmacists, dental clinics, and physiotherapists can bill the carrier directly, avoiding any manual claim submissions.

Q: Do I need separate insurance when traveling to other Canadian provinces?

A: Strongly recommended. While provincial health covers basic emergency doctor visits, ambulances, paramedical emergency services, and accidental dental are typically not covered out-of-province.
