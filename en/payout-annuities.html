1. Select Your Annuity Life Cycle

Single Life Annuity

Provides guaranteed payouts for your entire life. Payouts persist for as long as you live, even past age 110, completely eradicating the risk of outliving your capital reserves.

Joint Life Annuity

Guarantees payouts for two lives. Extremely ideal for spouses; income continues seamlessly until the last surviving spouse passes away.

Term Certain Annuity

Secures guaranteed payouts for a designated term (e.g. 10 or 20 years). If you pass away during this term, all remaining payments flow directly to your designated beneficiaries.

2. Configure Income Growth Patterns

  • ✓

    Level Payouts

    Income levels remain exactly constant across time, enabling absolute budget precision.

  • ✓

    Indexed Payouts

    Payments scale upward annually by a fixed percentage (e.g. 2% or 3%) to fight long-term inflation erosion.

  • ✓

    Integrated Payouts

    For early retirees. Payouts start higher to bridge the gap before government pensions (OAS/CPP) commence, then decrease slightly afterward to preserve a flat aggregate cash flow profile.

"What if I pass away shortly after purchasing an annuity?"

Understanding the Guaranteed Period Rider

This is a very common concern. To mitigate this scenario, you can easily attach a **"Guaranteed Period"** rider (e.g. 10 or 15 years) to your Single or Joint Life annuity contract.

If you pass away before the guaranteed period ends, the insurance carrier will **never** retain your capital! We continue monthly disbursements to your designated beneficiaries, or pay out the remaining discounted value as a lump-sum death benefit.
